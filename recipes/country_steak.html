Paul's Country Fried Steak®

Paul's Country Fried Steak® (pictured above) is a twist on the famous dish while keeping it a classic comfort food

Ingredients

  • Thin cut New York strip Steak
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Panko Bread Crumbs

Steps

    NOTE: Have your flour, eggs, and bread crumbs ready in bowls and steak seasoned to your preference

  1. Dip your New York Strip Steak in the bowl of eggs and make sure steak is covered generously
  2. After a applying a good coat it's time to dip the steak in the flour, make sure the steak is completely convered
  3. After applying flour, dip the flour covered steak in the eggs once again until completely covered
  4. Now that you have a good coat of egg its time to dip the steak in the bread crumbs, make sure to cover completely
  5. Once you have your steak properly covered it's time to cook, pour enough oil in a frying pan to cover the bottom by 1/4 inch
  6. Heat up oil to 350°F or when oil sizzles when you drop a little flour in it
  7. Place steak in the oil and cook each side for 2 minutes or until golden brown
  8. Let steak cool for 2 minutes Steak will be HOT!
  9. Now that you've let it cool -You're ready to enjoy!!
