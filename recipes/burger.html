Paul's Smash Burger

Pauls smash burger (pictured above) made with Wagyu Beef makes it stand above other burgers

Ingredients

  • Wagyu Beef
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Sliced cheese of your choice
  • Sliced Red Onions
  • Yum Yum Sauce
  • Spicy Mayo
  • Artisan Buns

Steps

NOTE: Make sure to have your beef seasoned and your toppings ready to apply before proceeding

  1. Toast your bun - (Having your bun perfectly toasted is super imporant)
  2. Apply the Wagyu Beef to a pan warmed up to medium high and cook one side until a good sear is visible
  3. Once sear is visible turn off heat competely and flip patty
  4. Apply cheese to patty and cover with pan lid to melt cheese and let patty rest
  5. While allowing the cheese to melt add spicy mayo and yum yum sauce to the toasted bun
  6. Add shredded lettuce to the bottom bun (putting it on the top bun you risk everything falling out when you top your burger)
  7. Add onions to bottom bun as well
  8. Now that the cheese is melted and your patty is rested add the patty to the bottom bun
  9. If you prefer to add more lettuce now is the time to add on top of the cheese so that it will hold to the cheese
  10. Finally it's time to top your burger - You've just made the World Famous Paul Smash Burger!
